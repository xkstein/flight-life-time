By subtracting this from your predicted lifespan, we can find the average ammount of time it'll cost you to take this flight. \begin{align*}\text{Average time lost by dying in a flight} &= x - \frac{20 \text{ million}-1}{20 \text{ million}}x \newline&= \frac{1}{20 \text{ million}}x\end{align*}
